by Santiago Orozco


Josefin Sans is a typeface that is geometric, elegant, and vintage. It was inspired by the geometric sans serif designs from the 1920s. Sans serif means without serifs. The x-height is halfway from the baseline to cap height. It also has a sister font called Josefin Slab. Santiago Orozco is a Type Designer based in Monterrey, Nuevo León, México. In 2009 he Founded Typemade Foundry/Studio, and specializes in Type Design, Font Production, and Type Technology.
